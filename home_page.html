What is Symptom Tracker?
This application allows people to more quickly and efficiently receive the proper medical attention they need. Being fueled by a sizeable medical database, holding information about almost all diseases, the application is trained to figure out what potential dangers you might be exposed to, and also to keep track of your medical history, enabling you to make better decisions regarding your health.
How to use it?
All you have to do is to press the "Go To Tracker" button at the top of the page and enter your year of birth and gender in the given boxes. Then you will be asked a series of questions which will eventually conclude your diagnosis. This will then be saved into you "Tracking History", where all of your diagnoses will be stored.
Why is it useful?
There are lots of people who go to the doctor for seemingly the silliest of reasons, like a mundane migraine or a mild pollen allergy. For these people, this app can prove itself to be very useful. Not only that, but it also makes medicine all the more accesible to as many people as possible, by launching the app as a free browser web application, that anyone with a simple device can access.
